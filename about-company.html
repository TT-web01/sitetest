Core Service Divisions

Legal Wing: Attorneys & Notary Publics

Our dedicated legal team provides comprehensive corporate law advisory, contract drafting and review, debt collection, and dispute resolution services. As registered Notary Publics, we also offer essential document certification and authentication.

Compliance Hub: PRAZ, NASSA, ZIMRA Specialists

This division is the cornerstone of regulatory adherence, specializing in vendor registration with PRAZ and NASSA, and ensuring full tax and customs compliance with ZIMRA. We keep you updated on the latest regulations to avoid penalties.

Business Solutions: Investment & Project Advisory

We offer strategic guidance on investment opportunities, project proposal development, order financing, and insurance. Our goal is to foster sustainable growth and maximize returns for your ventures.

Global Mobility: Immigration & Visa Experts

Facilitating seamless international transitions, this division handles all aspects of visa applications, study permits, police clearance, and even offers CoS brokering and university placement services.
